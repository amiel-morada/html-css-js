goSplit

A mobile app concept designed to simplify the process of splitting bills among friends, family, or colleagues. It features an intuitive interface that automatically calculates individual shares, including tips and taxes, making group payments quick and stress-free.

TakboLipa

A smart traffic management system tailored for the city of Lipa, aimed at reducing congestion and improving road safety. It uses real-time traffic data, adaptive signal controls, and predictive analytics to ensure smoother travel for commuters.

MusikaLog

An online logging system built for Musikalista to manage and track their events, performances, and schedules. It offers tools for storing setlists, recording rehearsal notes, and sharing updates with band members or collaborators.
